What is Fake news

Oftensensational news are created and spread through social media to achieve intended end. On the other hand, it may also involve narration of a true fact however being deliberately exaggerated. ... The problem is to identify the authenticity of the news .

Conclusion

This project aims to develop a method for detecting and classifying news stories using natural language processing. The main goal is to identify fake news, which is a classic text classification issue. We gathered our data, preprocessed the text, and translates our articles into supervised model features. Our goal is to develop a model that classifies a given news article as either fake or real.
